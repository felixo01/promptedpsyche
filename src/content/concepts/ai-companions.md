An AI companion is not the same as a therapist, friend or person. It is a system that can create a sense of presence, understanding or availability, while its behavior still depends on a model, interface and product design.

## Why it matters

- It connects conversational AI with emotion, routine and perceived availability.
- It helps distinguish user experience from the system's actual capacities.
- It raises questions about disclosure, dependency, boundaries and product design.
- It belongs in cyberpsychology because people can give systems social or relational roles.

## What not to simplify

AI companions should not be framed as therapy unless a source and product context clearly justify that framing. They should also not be treated as automatically harmful or automatically beneficial. The careful question is what role the system begins to play in a person's life.
