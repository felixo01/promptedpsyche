Human-AI Interaction is the study and practice of how people work with intelligent systems. For companies, it is where strategy becomes behavior.

An organization can buy access to powerful models and still fail to create useful AI practice. The limiting factor is often not model capability. It is the interaction between people, tools, incentives, norms and risk.

## What companies should notice

AI changes how people ask questions, produce drafts, check work, communicate uncertainty and assign responsibility. These changes can improve work, but they can also hide weak reasoning behind fluent output.

Human-AI Interaction gives teams a way to examine those moments before they become invisible habits.

## Practical value

Companies should care because good AI adoption depends on calibrated trust, clear review practices, psychologically realistic training and interfaces that support human judgment.

The question is not simply "which AI tool should we use?" It is "what kind of human behavior will this system produce?"
