AI-mediated communication describes communication between people that is generated, shaped, filtered or supported by AI systems. It includes situations where AI drafts a message, adjusts tone, recommends a reply, translates language or influences what one person sees from another.

## Why it matters

AI can change how people sound, what they disclose, how quickly they respond and how much responsibility they feel for a message. It can make communication smoother, but it can also blur authorship, sincerity and accountability.

This makes AI-mediated communication important for work, education, relationships, media and culture.

## What not to simplify

This is not only about chatbots. Often the conversation is still person-to-person, but AI sits in the middle as an assistant, editor, recommender or filter. The human relationship remains central, even when the wording or visibility of communication is shaped by a system.
