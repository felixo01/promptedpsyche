Cyberpsychology studies how digital environments influence human thought and behavior. The cyberpsychology of AI focuses on a newer question: what changes when digital environments become responsive, generative and socially persuasive?

AI systems can answer, remember, imitate, recommend, evaluate and converse. This gives them psychological weight. They become part of how people make sense of themselves, other people and the world.

## Core questions

- How does AI change attention, emotion and motivation?
- Why do people attribute understanding, care or authority to systems?
- How do AI-mediated relationships affect identity and communication?
- What happens to trust when answers feel personal and immediate?
- How do AI tools reshape learning, work and creativity?

## Why it matters

AI is not only a technical infrastructure. It is a psychological environment. Studying that environment helps us design better tools, healthier norms and more responsible organizations.
