# English Adaptation QA

Use this checklist before publishing English versions of Polish articles or notes.

- The English version reads as an adaptation, not a literal translation.
- The text does not feel machine-translated.
- Polish syntax is not copied into English sentence rhythm.
- English titles sound natural as essay or note titles.
- Concept links point to English concept pages.
- Polish and English versions preserve the same thesis.
- English versions may adapt sentence order and phrasing for clarity.
- Claims are careful and do not overstate evidence.
- The text does not imply peer review or formal research output by Feliks Mamczur.
- No fake DOI or placeholder DOI is added.
- No AI influencer, prompt-guru, SaaS or tool-reviewer tone appears.
