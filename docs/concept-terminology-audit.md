# Concept Terminology Audit

Short working note for public Polish concept names. This file is not part of the rendered Astro site.

| Public PL title | EN term | Category | Rationale | Supporting sources |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AI literacy | AI literacy | termin angielski używany w polskim kontekście | Polish public texts often keep `AI literacy` and explain it as `kompetencje w zakresie AI`. The title stays English and the description explains the Polish meaning. | PARP: https://en.parp.gov.pl/component/content/article/89129%3Aai-literacy-czyli-o-odpowiedzialnym-korzystaniu-ze-sztucznej-inteligencji; Gov.pl: https://www.gov.pl/web/cyfryzacja/pierwsze-przepisy-rozporzadzenia-o-sztucznej-inteligencji-ai-act-zaczynaja-obowiazywac |
| Human-AI Interaction | Human-AI Interaction | termin angielski używany w polskim kontekście | Polish sources use forms such as `interakcja człowiek-AI`, but the project already uses the English field name. The title stays English with a Polish explanation. | AGH BaDAP: https://badap.agh.edu.pl/autorzy/zespoly/badania-nad-relacja-czlowiek-ai-00471; NASK BIP: https://bip.nask.pl/bip/organizacja/2,Organizacja.html |
| Calibrated trust | Calibrated trust | brak stabilnego polskiego odpowiednika | Polish uses such as `skalibrowane zaufanie` or `kalibracja zaufania` appear, but not as a stable public term. The title stays English. | Example Polish use without evidence of a settled term: https://www.kasiaszczesna.pl/post/jak-budowac-zaufanie-w-systemach-ai-wyzwanie-projektowe-i-etyczne; source literature in Lee and See (2004). |
| Błąd automatyzacji | Automation bias | utrwalony polski termin | The Polish AI Act context and responsible AI glossary use `błąd automatyzacji`. | AI Act Service Desk: https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/pl/ai-act/article-14; Google Responsible AI glossary: https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/glossary/responsible-ai?hl=pl |
| Cognitive offloading | Cognitive offloading | brak stabilnego polskiego odpowiednika | `Poznawcze odciążanie` appears in Polish explanatory texts, but the English term remains clearer and less forced for a concept-library title. | Example Polish explanatory use: https://pruszczak.com/wplyw-ai-myslenie-krytyczne/ |
| Antropomorfizacja | Anthropomorphism | utrwalony polski termin | `Antropomorfizacja` is a standard Polish term and is used in Polish AI commentary and academic contexts. | No Fluff Jobs: https://nofluffjobs.com/pl/log/technologia-pl/antropomorfizacja-i-sztuczna-inteligencja/; Biblioteka Nauki: https://bibliotekanauki.pl/articles/55786237 |
| AI companions | AI companions | termin angielski używany w polskim kontekście | Polish texts use `towarzysze AI`, but often alongside the English term. The title stays English to avoid making the Polish phrase sound more settled than it is. | Goethe-Institut: https://www.goethe.de/ins/pl/pl/kul/med/rfn/syntetyczna-empatia.html; Internet Matters: https://www.internetmatters.org/pl/resources/ai-chatbots-and-virtual-friends-how-parents-can-keep-children-safe/ |
| Nadzór ze strony człowieka | Human oversight | utrwalony polski termin | This is the Polish regulatory wording used in the AI Act context. | AI Act Service Desk: https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/pl/ai-act/article-14 |
| Model output | Model output | brak stabilnego polskiego odpowiednika | Polish texts use descriptive phrases such as `odpowiedź modelu` and `output modelu`, but no stable term was identified. The title stays English and the description explains it. | Example Polish usage: https://sztucznainteligencja.org.pl/biecek-si-musimy-cos-sobie-wyjasnic/ |
| AI-mediated communication | AI-mediated communication | brak stabilnego polskiego odpowiednika | Polish academic and professional contexts mostly cite the English term or use descriptive phrases. The title stays English with a Polish explanation. | BazEkon record: https://bazekon.icm.edu.pl/bazekon/element/bwmeta1.element.ekon-element-000171717823; UW course context: https://informatorects.uw.edu.pl/switch_language?lang=pl&next=%2Fen%2Fcourses%2Fview%3Fprz_kod%3D3200-L2-NS-Hum |
