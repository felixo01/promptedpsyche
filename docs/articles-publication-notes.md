## DOI and citation policy

Prompted Psyche does not assign fake DOI identifiers.

A DOI can be added only after registration through a real registration route such as Crossref, DataCite, Zenodo or another legitimate repository or agency.

Until then, every public article should use:

- a visible author byline
- a canonical URL
- a suggested citation
- a rights notice under the article

The DOI field should not be displayed when no DOI exists. Do not show placeholder DOI text.

A DOI does not mean quality, review or peer-reviewed status by itself. It must not be used to create a false academic impression.
